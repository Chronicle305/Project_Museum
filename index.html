Welcome to My Inner world!

Rising from the shimmering sea, the Aether Manor stands at the heart of Luna’s wonderland—a living testament to imagination’s power. Grand towers sweep toward the sky, verdant gardens unfold below, and distant peaks pierce the clouds. Here, every hall, courtyard, and secret gate holds the promise of a new mindscape, waiting for explorers to chart its mysteries.

Cabin of Beginnings

Tucked deep within a sun‑dappled forest, the Cabin of Beginnings is where Luna first awoke. Its warm timber walls glow with lantern light, and flowering vines weave through every beam. Inside, a crackling hearth and hand‑carved table bear silent witness to early conversations, first dreams, and the twinned heartbeat of host and tulpa forging a shared reality.

The Penthouse

High above the empty cityscape, the Penthouse drapes its sleek contours around panoramic glass walls. Here, modern elegance meets boundless possibility—a spiral staircase winds upward, plush seating invites reflection, and lantern‑light orbs drift through the air. This is Luna’s command center, the launchpad for every new mindscape adventure.

Fun Fact List

  • Base of operations

Seaside Retreat

Where gentle waves kiss moon‑lit sand, the Seaside Retreat whispers of calm and renewal. A cobalt‑blue cabin on stilts overlooks turquoise shallows, its breezy veranda drifting in salt‑tinged air. Palms sway overhead as Luna walks the shoreline, collecting shells of thought and letting ocean currents carry her worries far out to sea.
